Virtual Exhibition 2023 -- Throes of Undertaking

I greet thee!

I began my studies in the Lapland University of Applied Sciences nearly four years ago in the fall 2019. And what a four years it has been! I was once barely a seedling of an artist who dreamed big but had little means to express it, neither the eye nor the ability to fullfill the desire to create.

But somewhere along the line however the more I tried turning my hobby into a full-time day work, I kind of got lost. Knowing I was supposed to make money off my art wasn't something of an motivator but rather an inhibitor. Not to mention numerous taboos, a great variety of them depending on which direction you're looking at geographically. Get too close in on any and you're cancelled out for life. I don't know when but at some point I began thinking of the whole art world as something of a battlefield.

I've always wished to be a versatile artist. Be it style-wise or theme-wise, I've likely tried it all. I've compiled some works I've made spring together as part of this virtual exhibition. Many of the artworks represented have multiple layers of meaning. Not one of them is exactly the same, and I've quite liberally tried my hand at different ways and styles of expression.

Without further ado, I represent the artworks down below. Do note that some of them may not be suitable to young audiences.
