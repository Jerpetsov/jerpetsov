Virtual Exhibition 2023 -- Throes of Undertaking

I began my studies in the Lapland University of Applied Sciences nearly four years ago in the fall 2019. I was barely a seedling of an artist who dreamed big but had neither the ability nor the eye to fulfill the desire to create. Really, I couldn't really even draw.

There have been times when I've been sure I would never create art for living. Often have I lost my way, often have I stumbled, and often enough have I picked up a pencil and begun anew. While I cannot tell where I'll end up, I can at least tell where I have been.

I've always wished to be a versatile artist so I find it proper that I would include artworks of different styles I've created during this spring. Some artworks are more abstract in their nature, some more figurative in their display. I've explored great a many different themes, and great a variety of venues in how I'd best like conducting myself in the exploration of the world of art the best. Behold, here lie the artworks.
